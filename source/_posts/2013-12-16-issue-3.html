How PayPal Created A Private Cloud And Switched To Agile

Tagged: techweekly, paypal, agile, cloud
In a world where new payment players are popping up like mushrooms on a dead tree in autumn, PayPal faced a big challenge: staying flexible and fast, while serving 130 million accounts and managing 13k employees. Their secret sauce included: top-down support, setting up a large private cloud using OpenStack and introducing a DevOps culture.

Employee Retention

Tagged: techweekly, hr, startup
Any entrepreneur in technology and particular in software will tell you: it's hard to find good developers, really hard. In this article, @sama discusses 3 good ways to retain talent: give employees a sense of mission, this gives everybody a feeling they are working on something important. Second retention strategy: rocketship growth. It's fun, and means everybody is constantly exposed to new challenges. Third: provide a great working environment, meaning good cultural values and a stellar team.
